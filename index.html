Miss Chicku

Thinking about you makes my soul come alive, and my heart can’t stop fluttering when you are next to me. Those expressions when you smile are so pleasing to the eyes and it feels like I have achieved everything. The hair on you suits you perfectly and adds more stars to the happiness on your face. It just suits you so well that sometimes I feel like running my fingers through your hair. I know it sounds weird, but I am just being honest. You know some people say that “ is the happiest place on the earth”, I doubt they never stood/ sat next to you. I don’t know, whether there’s an airport nearby or my heart is just taking off.you’re always there when I need you, and you always know how to make me feel special. I love you more than anything, my sweetheart. Chetana, you are my heart and soul. Seeing you smile, hearing you laugh, and feeling your touch is all I need to be happy.
